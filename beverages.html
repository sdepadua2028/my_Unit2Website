Beverages


Calamansi Juice

Calamansi is a citrus hybrid that tastes like the combination of a lime and an orange or tangerine. This fruit is native to many parts of Southeast Asia, and is often mixed with water and/or sugar to create this refreshing drink. Calamansi juice can also be made warm to soothe the throat and boost immunity.

(Photo from "Calamansi Juice" by Foxy Folksy )

Sago't Gulaman

"Sago't gulaman" translates to "tapioca pearls and jelly/gelatin". It is an iced beverage that is made from tapioca pearls, gelatin (usually red), and brown sugar syrup. Sometimes, it is mixed with banana extract (or other variations) to enhance the flavor and give it a nice aroma.

(Photo from "Sago't Gulaman" by Food with Mae )

Melon Drink

This Filipino melon drink is traditionally made of shredded cantaloupe, water, and sugar, but milk can also be added for a sweeter and more creamy flavor. It is a popular street drink in the Philippines, sold in plastic bags with straws, along with sago't gulaman and coconut water.
